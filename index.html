LJ STAND

// About

LJ STAND is a team of enthusiastic students from Brisbane Boys' College, Australia. We work hard to do the best we can and strive for excellence in the Robocup Junior competition.


Last year saw us acheive first place at the Robocup Junior Australian national competition, qualifying us for the 2017 international competition in Nagoya, Japan.


This year we will be building two robots, with the intention of competing at the Robocup Junior regional, state, national and possibly international competitions. Over the course of the year leading up to the competitions, we will be designing, building and programming our robot to play in the lightweight soccer division.

// Team

LJ STAND consists of 5 members: Lachlan Grant, James Yelland, James Wallington, Sam Tudor and Nakul Doshi.
