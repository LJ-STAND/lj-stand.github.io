Who We Are

LJ-STAND is a team of enthusiastic students from Brisbane Boys' College, Australia. We work hard to do the best we can and strive for excellence in the Robocup Junior competition.

What We Do

This year we will be building two robots\, with the intention of competing at the Robocup Junior regional, state and national competitions. Over the course of the year leading up to the competitions, we will be designing, building and programming our robot to play in the lightweight soccer division.
